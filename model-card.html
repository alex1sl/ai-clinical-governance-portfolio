Technical Model Card: HEDIS MY 2026

Model Details

  • Model Type: LLM (Optimized for Clinical Summarization)
  • Framework Alignment: NIST RMF 1.0
  • Governance Lead: Alexis Smith, NP

Intended Use

This model is intended to assist in identifying 30-day follow-up windows for asthma patients to meet HEDIS quality benchmarks.

Out of Scope

This model is NOT for autonomous clinical diagnosis or medication adjustment. All outputs are advisory and require HITL validation.

Risk & Mitigation: Chronological Logic

Testing revealed significant errors during Leap Year cycles and holiday weekends.

Risk Identified Governance Control
30-Day Window Breach 28-Day Threshold Flag for HITL review.
Date Hallucination SQL-based audit of processed dates vs. raw data.
